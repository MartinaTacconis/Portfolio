Who I am

I’m a psychology graduate and digital marketer who loves understanding what people need and creating digital experiences—content, campaigns, and partnerships—that make their journey easier and more engaging.

My work sits at the intersection of content, growth, and partnerships, from acquisition and activation experiments to influencer collaborations and community-building.
