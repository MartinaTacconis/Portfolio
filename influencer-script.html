Influencer Campaign Script

An influencer script tailored for Italian students: from hook and structure on the left, to key points and skills demonstrated on the right.

Script shared with creators

Context: Italian university students · Return from exam season

Translation of original Italian script

You’ve just wrapped up exam season and now it’s time to dive back into uni… but your budget’s feeling a little tight? Need to stock up on everything without draining your wallet?

Well, have you ever tried cashback?

Cashback is one of the easiest ways to save money when you shop online. Every time you buy something, a portion of what you’ve spent gets paid back to you. Simple.

There are plenty of cashback sites out there, but TopCashback is completely free and incredibly easy to use, ideal for us students! And as the name suggests, it offers some of the highest cashback rates you’ll find anywhere. No wonder it’s a global leader with over 20 million members.

And right now, until the 4th of March, there’s an unmissible offer bundle across all the stores you’ll probably need for the start of term or just to simply treat yourself, like Just Eat, Privè, Espresso, Unieuro and Libraccio with cashback rates going up to 50%!

If you’re curious and want to start saving on your favourite brands, just click the link below.

And don’t forget to follow TopCashback Italy to stay up to date with all the latest deals.

See you there!

Key points

  • Framed the script around a real student moment (post-exam, low budget, needing to “restart” the semester) to create instant emotional relevance.
  • Used a clear problem–solution structure: tight budget and essentials to buy → cashback as the easiest way to save.
  • Combined education and simplicity by explaining cashback in one clear sentence.
  • Added social proof and credibility (20M+ members, high cashback rates) to build trust quickly.
  • Introduced urgency with a time-bound offer (“until the 4th of March”) to encourage immediate action.
  • Made the benefits concrete by referencing relevant brands (Just Eat, Privè, Espresso, Unieuro, Libraccio) and a value anchor (“up to 50% cashback”).
  • Closed with a dual call to action: click the link to start saving now + follow TopCashback Italy for ongoing deals.

Skills demonstrated

  • Audience insight and positioning specifically for students.
  • Writing contextual hooks that fit influencer / TikTok style.
  • Explaining a financial product in simple, conversational language.
  • Using persuasion levers (social proof, urgency, value anchor) in a transparent, non-pushy way.
  • Briefing creators with a script that respects their community while aligning with brand goals.
